About Dakota

Handcrafted with Love in Corydon, Indiana

Hi there! I'm Dakota Wright, and I'm passionate about creating beautiful, high-quality collars for your beloved pets.

Each collar is handcrafted with careful attention to detail, using only the best materials to ensure comfort and durability for your furry friends.

What started as a hobby making collars for my own pets quickly grew into a small business as friends and family began requesting custom pieces. Now, I'm proud to offer my handmade pet collars to pet lovers throughout Corydon and beyond.

Most of my customers are local, but I'm happy to ship nationwide for those who fall in love with my designs from afar!
